about

Daisuke Fujita (Meitei / 冥丁) is a musical artist from Hiroshima, Japan. He released his first album Kwaidan (Ghost Stories) in 2018. His atmospheric works attempt to recapture what he calls a "Lost Japanese Mood," sampling old-style vocals and traditional Japanese instruments from roughly a hundred years past.

His albums have featured on Pitchfork's "best of" lists, and he has received strong praise from other publications and music enthusiasts. Below, you will find his albums and social media accounts, as well as reviews and interviews.
